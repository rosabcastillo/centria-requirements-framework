## Governance Layer
Architecture (Solution, Infrastructure, Logical) | Feature Prioritization | AI Governance | IT Resource Alignment

**Feature Prioritization process:**
- CC ESC — new epics/features get approved here
- Large cross-functional programs Steercos (SOC, ERM, etc.) — new features approved here
- Departmental enhancements & production support — prioritized directly with IT SME

**Architecture & IT Resource Alignment:** Weekly 2-hour meeting
- Hour 1: speed dating on the solution approach
- Hour 2: complex architecture discussions and resource re-alignment
- Output: final architecture and resource blueprint
